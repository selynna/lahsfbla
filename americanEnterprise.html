The American Enterprise Project

  • What is the American Enterprise Project?

    • The American Enterprise Project aims to educate students and the community about the America's free-market system. Our economic system is based on these five pillars: freedom of choice, the right to private property, the right to private enterprise, competition, and profit. FBLA members explore business participation in the economy and the role of government regulation. From the American Enterprise Project, students gain an understanding of the larger economic (and sometimes political) scene.

  • Want to get involved?

    • Check the calendar for any upcoming American Enterprise (AE) guest talks, business tours, or career opportunities. FBLA members interested in helping organize the project can contact the Director of American Enterprise, Anisha Palasamudrum.
